About Me

Crafting digital
experiences that

I'm Hafijur Rahman Sajib, a UI/UX Designer based in Khulna, Bangladesh, specializing in SaaS, Fintech & E-Commerce products. I bridge the gap between business goals and user needs through thoughtful, research-driven design.

My design process is rooted in empathy and data. Every interface I create is intentional — optimized for conversion, built for usability, and crafted with pixel-perfect attention to detail.
